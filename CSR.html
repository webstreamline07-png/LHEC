Tourism & Tiaki Promise

Welcoming Aotearoa

Manaakitanga

Delivering Authentic
New Zealand Hospitality

Manaakitanga represents the Māori tradition of hospitality and kindness. At Lower Hutt Events Centre, we honour this principle by welcoming every guest with genuine warmth and care, sharing the wonder of Lower Hutt's natural beauty and helping visitors discover authentic experiences. This deeply rooted value shapes every interaction, from our first greeting to our fond farewells, ensuring that each guest feels the true spirit of New Zealand hospitality throughout their visit.

Language & Identity

Te Reo Māori
& Sign Language

We embrace New Zealand's cultural identity by incorporating Te Reo Māori and Māori sign language when ordering and interacting with clients. This commitment ensures we honour our country's indigenous heritage and create inclusive experiences for all guests. By integrating these languages into our daily operations, we celebrate the richness of Aotearoa's diverse culture whilst ensuring accessibility and respect for all who visit our venue.

Environmental Sustainability

Guardians of the Land

Kaitiakitanga

Protecting Our
Natural Heritage

Kaitiakitanga embodies the Māori concept of environmental stewardship. Positioned in the heart of Lower Hutt, we recognise our responsibility as caretakers of this extraordinary landscape. We implement sustainable practices and support local conservation efforts, ensuring the breathtaking scenery our guests cherish remains unspoilt for future visitors.

Operations

Greener Ground
Operations

At the foundational level, we've implemented essential switches in our everyday operations to reduce water, waste, and energy consumption. We've transitioned to LED lighting and sensor-based systems throughout the venue. Our water conservation efforts include sensor-based faucets and full load dishwasher practices in the kitchen, taking meaningful steps to reduce our carbon footprint.

Waste Management

Zero Food Waste
Commitment

Lower Hutt Events Centre proudly maintains a zero-tolerance approach to food wastage. We rescue and redistribute food through strategic partnerships with Kaibosh and Capital Compost. Our Tūtaki Café has eliminated single-use plastics entirely, and we exclusively use recyclable coffee cups and compostable packaging. Through comprehensive waste segregation practices, we ensure efficient recycling and composting across all operations.

Innovation

Digital
Operations

We've eliminated pen and paper from our operations, transitioning completely to digital systems across all departments. This significant initiative reduces our environmental impact by saving thousands of sheets of paper annually whilst simultaneously improving operational efficiency and data management. By embracing digital-first practices, we demonstrate that sustainability and innovation go hand in hand.

Partnerships

Responsible Partnerships

We prioritise partnerships with organisations like Capital Compost, Kaibosh, and local food suppliers to amplify our sustainable operations and create meaningful community impact. Our event partners share our commitment to sustainability, opting for reusable décor and collaborating with us to develop customised, eco-friendly gifting and decoration solutions. These collaborative relationships enable us to extend our environmental values beyond our venue walls.

People & Community

Giving Back

Healthcare Support

Te Omanga
Hospice Support

We make regular annual contributions to Te Omanga Hospice by serving as kitchen helpers, providing hands-on support to this vital community healthcare service. Our team dedicates time and effort to assist with meal preparation, demonstrating our commitment to giving back to those who care for our community's most vulnerable members.

Welfare

Food Redistribution
Programme

Any surplus food that is not suitable for donation is responsibly utilised by serving it to our employees and their families. This practice not only minimises food wastage but also fosters a genuine sense of community within our team, demonstrating that sustainability and employee wellbeing are interconnected values.

Community

Community
Initiatives

We actively support causes that matter to our community, believing that hospitality extends far beyond exceptional service. During August, we proudly supported the Cancer Society's Daffodil Day initiative, with our team wearing daffodil pins, offering daffodil-shaped biscuits and fresh blooms to guests, and helping to raise much-needed funds for those in need.

Culinary Culture

Flavours of
the World

Through special promotions celebrating international cuisine — including bento boxes, chaat and Indian thali, French crêpes, and Southern BBQ — we spread our philosophy of manaakitanga through meaningful culinary experiences. These initiatives have received overwhelming support, demonstrating how food brings people together across cultures.

Team & Culture

Our People

Development

Staff Training
& Awareness

We place strong emphasis on fostering staff awareness about sustainable practices through meticulously arranged training sessions that empower our team with knowledge and skills. By equipping our staff with comprehensive understanding of sustainability initiatives, we ensure our commitment to environmental and social responsibility is reflected in every aspect of our operations.

Wellbeing

Employee
Support System

We understand that work and life can sometimes present challenges, which is why we provide easy access to contact information and resources for employees who may need help. Our comprehensive support system ensures team members have the resources, guidance, and assistance they need to navigate difficulties and thrive in their roles.

Team Spirit

Monthly Team Social Gatherings

We host monthly social gatherings to foster a sense of community and celebrate our team's collective achievements in a relaxed, enjoyable atmosphere. These events provide valuable opportunities for staff to unwind, socialise, and build stronger connections with colleagues across departments. During these gatherings, we recognise our Employees of the Month, celebrating their exceptional hard work and dedication.
