Getting Here

The Lower Hutt Events Centre is only 20 minutes drive from Central Wellington.


Arriving from Wellington

Exit Wellington via SH2 keeping to the right two lanes on the Harbour side of the motorway. Drive for 12 minutes (off peak and 20 minutes during peak hour) past the Petone off ramp and take the next exit (Maungaraki). Then turn right at the roundabout into Lower Hutt. Continue in the right lane onto Hutt Road and follow up and over the Ewen bridge taking a right at the roundabout onto Woburn Road. Continue for 200m and turn left on to Myrtle Street. Drive 500m and take the first left to reach the Lower Hutt Events Centre.


Arriving from the North on SH1

On SH1 at Pukerua Bay continue for a further 6km and then turn left at the traffic lights onto Grays Road. Follow the winding Grays Road for 6km and turn right onto Paekakariki Hill Road. Continue onto SH58 for 10km until you arrive at the Manor Park intersection – turn right onto SH2. Continue for 8km and take a left into Lower Hutt using the bridge. Turn right at the second roundabout onto High St and drive 900m before taking the 3rd turnoff on your left onto Laings road. Drive 250m along and the Lower Hutt Events centre will be to your right.


Arriving from Transmission Gully

Start at Transmission gully head southeast on motorway SH1. Follow SH1 and then at the roundabout take 1st exit onto SH58. Continue SH58, Haywards Hill Road (approx.10Km) Until you arrive at the Manor park Intersection. Merge onto SH2. At the roundabout take the 2nd exit onto the Hutt Valley (approx. 8KM) SH2 ramp. Continue SH2 towards Lower Hutt. Turn Left onto Melling Link using a bridge. Turn right at the second roundabout onto High St and drive 900m before taking the 3rd turnoff on your left onto Laing’s Road. Drive 250m along and the Lower Hutt Events Centre will be to your right.
