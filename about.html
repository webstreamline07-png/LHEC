Our Story

Lower Hutt's New Meeting Place

Lower Hutt Events Centre is the region's premier events destination — a modern, fully equipped facility nestled along the banks of Te Awa Kairangi in the heart of Wellington.

We host conferences, weddings, concerts, trade shows, gala dinners, and community gatherings with the same commitment to care and quality. Our team is driven by the spirit of manaakitanga — genuine hospitality that makes every guest and client feel truly welcomed.

From our award-winning culinary team to our expert event coordinators, we work as one to ensure every event we host is extraordinary.

Manaakitanga — genuine care and hospitality in every interaction
QualMark Gold Certified — New Zealand's tourism quality assurance standard
Community at heart — proudly serving Lower Hutt and the wider Wellington region
