Along Te Awa Kairangi, the Lower Hutt Events Centre is where ideas, people, and moments meet.

Guided by manaakitanga, every gathering is shaped with care.

In the heart of Lower Hutt, this is a place where people come together — to share ideas, mark milestones, and create memories that linger long after the day is done. Over the years, the Events Centre has become part of the city's rhythm, shaped by the people who gather here and the moments they bring with them.

As part of a connected precinct, it offers a setting where stays, dining, and celebrations flow naturally into one another, creating experiences that feel effortless, welcoming, and complete.
